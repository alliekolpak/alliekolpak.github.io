2017 DESIGN BRIEF: AUGMENTED REALITY

C O N C L U S I O N

Augmented reality has been around for several years and is already helping consumers live their lives easier. This industry has the potential to take off and become a huge part of everyday life. If prices go down then more people will be able to use these products. Augmented reality will help people make everyday jobs and tasks easier. Those scenes in Star Wars with information popping up into thin air are not that far away.
