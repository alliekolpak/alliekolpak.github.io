ARAPAHOE WARRIOR STEM

EXCELLENCE. TRADITION. INNOVATION.


"Our goal, while ensuring the same curriculum in all sections of these courses, is to support innovative teachers to teach using 21st Century technologies...AHS teachers will make a concerted effort to use the Internet, and a wide array of exciting online instructional resources, to facilitate innovative and empowering learning opportunities for their students." -AHS Principal Natalie Pramenko

"Students need access to the tools of the modern learner. We feel that to be successful in the 21st century, our students are going to need some different skills, abilities and habits of mind than we did in the last century. They will need to know how to be Connected Learners, able to create and maintain their own personal learning networks." -AHS Director of Technology Karl Fisch

Website Information

This website was created for the Webmaster competition for the Technology Student Association 2016-2017 school year conference. Its purpose is to educate on the STEM offerings at Arapahoe High School, improve awareness of our TSA chapter, and inform on augmented reality.
