2017 DESIGN BRIEF: AUGMENTED REALITY

What is augmented reality?

Google would define augmented reality as, “a technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view.” This is just a fancy way of saying using technology to put digital and virtual information into the real world. For example if there is a stargazing app where the phone is pointed into the sky and the phone will display information about the stars in the sky in front of the phone camera. It is also like a heads up display that will display information about surroundings on a screen.
